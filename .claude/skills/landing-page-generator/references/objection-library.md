# Objection Library

Real objections grouped by category. For the page's FAQ, pick the 4–6 that actually apply to this offer. Don't make up objections.

For FAQ writing pattern (Q/A format, "even if" framing, banned reassurances), see `copy-rules.md`.

---

## Category 1 — Price / value

**Real objections:**
- "It's too expensive."
- "I can't justify this to my boss/team."
- "The free alternative is good enough."
- "I'll just build this myself."

**How to dismantle:**
- Frame against the cost of the *current* problem in their currency (hours, $, hires avoided).
- Show ROI math, not list price defense.
- Compare to the realistic alternative — including build-it-yourself in eng-hours.
- Offer a free trial, free tier, or money-back guarantee.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"How does this compare to building it ourselves?"*
  A: A typical implementation runs ~6–10 engineering weeks. We're $X/month, ship in a day, and maintain it for you. Most teams expense it from the saved engineering time.
- Q: *"Why is this more expensive than [competitor]?"*
  A: We're priced for teams with 10+ tickets/day — we include [specific feature] which their plan adds at $X/seat. If you're under 10 tickets/day, [competitor] is the better fit.

## Category 2 — Trust / credibility

**Real objections:**
- "Who built this — are they real?"
- "Is this a fly-by-night startup?"
- "What if you go out of business?"
- "Has anyone real used this?"

**How to dismantle:**
- Founder credentials, prior companies, named team.
- Customer logos, named testimonials.
- Funding (if relevant — series-stage signals stability for B2B).
- Data export / portability commitments.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"What happens if you shut down?"*
  A: Your data is exportable in standard formats anytime. We're profitable as of [year]; even in the worst case, you'd have 90 days notice and a one-click export.
- Q: *"Who's behind this?"*
  A: Built by [founder name], previously [prior role]. Team of [N], backed by [investors, if applicable]. Live since [date] with [N] active customers.

## Category 3 — Fit / "is this for me?"

**Real objections:**
- "I'm not technical — will I be able to use this?"
- "We're too small / too big for this."
- "Our use case is unusual."
- "We don't have time to onboard."

**How to dismantle:**
- Specific examples of similar customers.
- Onboarding time frame ("most teams are live in 20 minutes").
- Concierge/white-glove option for the high-touch case.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"Will this work for a 3-person team?"*
  A: Yes — about 40% of our customers are under 10 people. The starter plan is built for that scale. Everything works at one user; no minimum.
- Q: *"What if my team isn't technical?"*
  A: No code or setup required. The team at [example customer], a non-technical marketing team, was running campaigns within an hour.

## Category 4 — Effort / switching cost

**Real objections:**
- "How long does setup take?"
- "I'd have to migrate from [current tool]."
- "I don't want to learn another tool."
- "What about all my existing data/workflows?"

**How to dismantle:**
- Concrete setup time ("5 minutes," "1 day").
- Native integrations with the tools they already use.
- Migration help / import tools.
- Free onboarding for paid plans.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"How do I migrate from [existing tool]?"*
  A: One-click import for [X, Y, Z]. For others, we'll do the migration for you on Pro+ plans — typically 2–3 days end to end.
- Q: *"How long until we're up and running?"*
  A: Most teams ship their first [X] within 20 minutes of signing up. Full onboarding for a 50-person org averages 2 weeks with our team's help.

## Category 5 — Security / compliance (B2B critical)

**Real objections:**
- "Is my data safe?"
- "Do you have SOC 2 / GDPR / HIPAA?"
- "Can I host this on-prem / in our cloud?"
- "Who has access to our data?"

**How to dismantle:**
- Name specific certifications.
- Data residency options.
- Encryption at rest and in transit.
- Access controls (SSO, role-based permissions, audit logs).
- Link to security/trust page.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"Are you SOC 2 compliant?"*
  A: Yes — SOC 2 Type II, audited annually. GDPR-compliant with EU data residency available on Enterprise. Full security overview at [link].
- Q: *"Where is our data stored?"*
  A: AWS [region]. Encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). EU residency available on request.

## Category 6 — Outcome / "will it actually work?"

**Real objections:**
- "Will this really save me time / make me money?"
- "What if it doesn't work for my use case?"
- "I've tried tools like this before and they didn't deliver."

**How to dismantle:**
- Specific named case study with metrics.
- Free trial / pilot program.
- Money-back guarantee.
- "If it doesn't [specific outcome] in [timeframe], we'll [refund / extend / help]."

**Examples:**
- Q: *"What if it doesn't work for my team?"*
  A: 14-day free trial, no card required. If you're not seeing results by day 10, message us and we'll either fix it or extend the trial. ~30% of teams get extensions; ~95% of those convert.
- Q: *"I've tried similar tools before. How is this different?"*
  A: Most tools in this space [common limitation]. We do [specific differentiator] — see [named customer] who tried 3 alternatives before us; they cut [metric] by [X%] in [timeframe].

---

## Category 7 — Support / what happens after

**Real objections:**
- "What if I get stuck?"
- "Is there a real person to talk to?"
- "Will you keep adding features?"

**How to dismantle:**
- Named support channels and response times.
- Roadmap visibility.
- Founder access on smaller plans.

**Example:**
- Q: *"What kind of support do I get?"*
  A: Live chat with response in <2 hours during business hours, on every plan. Pro and above get a Slack channel with the team. Self-serve docs cover ~80% of questions.

## Picking the right 4–6 for the page

**B2B SaaS:** Price, Security, Setup time, "It works" proof.
**Consumer:** Refund/cancel, "Is this safe to buy from?", How long until I see results, Comparison vs alternative.
**Enterprise:** Security, On-prem option, Compliance, Data ownership, Migration support.
**Developer tools:** Pricing model, Open source / vendor lock-in, Self-host option, Reliability/uptime.

**Order them** per the rule in `conversion-framework.md` Section 8: setup early (momentum), trust mid, **price last** (the final wall).
