# Objection Library

Real objections by category. For the page's FAQ, pick the 4–6 that actually apply. Don't invent objections.

For Q/A writing pattern (format, "even if" framing, banned reassurances), see `copy-rules.md`.

---

## 1. Price / value

**Objections:** "It's too expensive." / "Can't justify to my boss." / "Free alternative is good enough." / "I'll build it myself."

**Dismantle:**
- Frame against the cost of the *current* problem in their currency (hours, $, hires avoided).
- ROI math, not list-price defense.
- Compare to realistic alternatives, including build-it-yourself in eng-hours.
- Free trial, free tier, or money-back guarantee.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"How does this compare to building it ourselves?"*
  A: A typical implementation runs ~6–10 engineering weeks. We're $X/month, ship in a day, maintain it for you. Most teams expense it from saved engineering time.
- Q: *"Why is this more expensive than [competitor]?"*
  A: We're priced for teams with 10+ tickets/day — we include [specific feature] which their plan adds at $X/seat. Under 10 tickets/day, [competitor] is the better fit.

## 2. Trust / credibility

**Objections:** "Who built this?" / "Fly-by-night startup?" / "What if you go out of business?" / "Has anyone real used this?"

**Dismantle:**
- Founder credentials, prior companies, named team.
- Customer logos, named testimonials.
- Funding signals (when relevant for B2B).
- Data export / portability commitments.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"What happens if you shut down?"*
  A: Your data is exportable in standard formats anytime. We're profitable as of [year]; even worst-case, you'd have 90 days notice and one-click export.
- Q: *"Who's behind this?"*
  A: Built by [founder], previously [prior role]. Team of [N], backed by [investors]. Live since [date] with [N] active customers.

---

## 3. Fit

**Objections:** "I'm not technical." / "We're too small / too big." / "Our use case is unusual." / "We don't have time to onboard."

**Dismantle:**
- Specific examples of similar customers.
- Onboarding time frame.
- Concierge option for high-touch cases.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"Will this work for a 3-person team?"*
  A: Yes — ~40% of our customers are under 10 people. Starter plan is built for that scale. Everything works at one user; no minimum.
- Q: *"What if my team isn't technical?"*
  A: No code or setup required. The team at [example customer], a non-technical marketing team, was running campaigns within an hour.

## 4. Effort / switching cost

**Objections:** "How long does setup take?" / "I'd have to migrate." / "Don't want to learn another tool." / "What about my existing data?"

**Dismantle:**
- Concrete setup time ("5 minutes," "1 day").
- Native integrations with their existing tools.
- Migration help / import tools.
- Free onboarding for paid plans.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"How do I migrate from [existing tool]?"*
  A: One-click import for [X, Y, Z]. For others, we'll do the migration on Pro+ plans — typically 2–3 days end to end.
- Q: *"How long until we're up and running?"*
  A: Most teams ship their first [X] within 20 minutes of signing up. Full onboarding for a 50-person org averages 2 weeks with our team's help.

## 5. Security / compliance (B2B critical)

**Objections:** "Is my data safe?" / "Do you have SOC 2 / GDPR / HIPAA?" / "Can I host on-prem?" / "Who has access to our data?"

**Dismantle:**
- Specific certifications.
- Data residency options.
- Encryption at rest and in transit.
- Access controls (SSO, role-based, audit logs).
- Link to security/trust page.

**Examples:**
- Q: *"Are you SOC 2 compliant?"*
  A: Yes — SOC 2 Type II, audited annually. GDPR-compliant with EU data residency on Enterprise. Full overview at [link].
- Q: *"Where is our data stored?"*
  A: AWS [region]. Encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). EU residency on request.

## 6. Outcome

**Objections:** "Will this really save me time / make me money?" / "What if it doesn't work for my use case?" / "I've tried tools like this before."

**Dismantle:**
- Specific named case study with metrics.
- Free trial / pilot.
- Money-back guarantee.
- "If it doesn't [outcome] in [timeframe], we'll [refund / extend]."

**Examples:**
- Q: *"What if it doesn't work for my team?"*
  A: 14-day free trial, no card required. If you're not seeing results by day 10, message us — we'll fix it or extend the trial. ~30% of teams get extensions; ~95% of those convert.
- Q: *"I've tried similar tools. How is this different?"*
  A: Most tools in this space [common limitation]. We do [specific differentiator] — see [named customer] who tried 3 alternatives before us; they cut [metric] by [X%] in [timeframe].

---

## 7. Support

**Objections:** "What if I get stuck?" / "Is there a real person to talk to?" / "Will you keep adding features?"

**Dismantle:**
- Named support channels and response times.
- Roadmap visibility.
- Founder access on smaller plans.

**Example:**
- Q: *"What kind of support do I get?"*
  A: Live chat, <2 hour response during business hours, on every plan. Pro and above get a Slack channel with the team. Self-serve docs cover ~80% of questions.

## Picking 4–6 for the page

- **B2B SaaS:** Price, Security, Setup time, "It works" proof.
- **Consumer:** Refund/cancel, "Safe to buy?", Time to results, Comparison vs alternative.
- **Enterprise:** Security, On-prem, Compliance, Data ownership, Migration.
- **Developer tools:** Pricing model, OSS / vendor lock-in, Self-host, Reliability/uptime.

**Order:** setup early (momentum), trust mid, **price last** (the final wall).
